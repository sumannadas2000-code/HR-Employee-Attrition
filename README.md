# HR-Employee-Attrition
HR Attrition interactive report on power bi
Insight:
<br>
Employee attrition analysis shows that most employees are leaving due to overtime workload, low salary hikes, and poor work-life balance. Employees with lower job satisfaction and limited promotion opportunities have a higher attrition rate. Younger employees and those with fewer years of experience are more likely to leave the organization. Certain job roles with high stress and low engagement contribute significantly to attrition. Improving employee satisfaction, career growth, and workplace culture can help reduce employee turnover.
